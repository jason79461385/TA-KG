The Matrix is a 1999 science fiction action film. 
It stars Keanu Reeves as Neo, who discovers that humanity is trapped inside a simulated reality.
Laurence Fishburne plays Morpheus, and Carrie-Anne Moss is Trinity.
Hugo Weaving plays the antagonist, Agent Smith.

Tom Hanks played a simple man with a great heart in Forrest Gump (1994).
The movie was directed by Robert Zemeckis.

Tom Hanks also starred in The Green Mile (1999) as Paul Edgecomb.
Interestingly, Hugo Weaving (famous for The Matrix) also appeared in The Green Mile as a Prison Guard.
This creates a connection between the world of The Matrix and The Green Mile.

Finally, Tom Hanks and Robert Zemeckis collaborated again on Cast Away (2000), where Hanks plays Chuck Noland.
Tom Hanks also starred in the romantic comedy You've Got Mail (1998).
